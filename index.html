Who are we?

We are the order of the New Genesis and we strive to reach the equilibrity, that is Gods will. We wish for a better world and nothing more.

Listen to the messages of God and let them guide your heart to the light. The darkness that has decended upon this earth and crept into our daily lives, shall be vanquished. We will purge those who stand in the way of His will. Purge! Renew! Redeem!

Info

We were founded at the beginning of "The war to end all wars" (01.01.1915), but this did not stay true. We were founded to help those scarred by this war, but it didn't stop, so we changed direction.

We are going to change this world and make them see the truth behind the curtain. This world is rotten, covered by a layer of evil and darkness, that has shrouded our hearts.
